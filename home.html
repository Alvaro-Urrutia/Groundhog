Welcome to the Groundhog Project

Groundhog is a SketchUp extension that intends to simplify the creation of Radiance models for simulation and rendering. Its development started during my M.Sc. thesis, which was focused on performing integrated lighting and thermal simulations of spaces with complex fenestration systems using Radiance and EnergyPlus. Since the thesis was focused in complex fenestration systems and shading devices, a few special features were needed. Accordingly, Groundhog is focused on Three and Five phase methods, although it also allows exporting models for rendering. It lets you use your own material library, and if the material assigned is not in a library, it assignes a Plastic material with the corresponding color.

This is an OpenSource project that allow people to modify the Code as well as the documentation, hoping to obtain more and better resources for everyone.
