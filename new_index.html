    Groundhog

    Lighting analysis within SketchUp

    What is it

    What is Groundhog?

    Groundhog is an Free and OpenSource SketchUp extention that allows creating Radiance models for lighting analysis.

    Features

    What can it do?

    We believe that layers, groups and components are not randomly chosen and views are not saved arbitrarily, Groundhog exports your model as you meant it to be.
